Overview

Kannada Flashcards is a simple educational app that helps users learn Kannada vocabulary. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (if any) and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact details are collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting is sent to any server.
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers are included.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store minimal local preferences (such as your last viewed card) solely on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.
